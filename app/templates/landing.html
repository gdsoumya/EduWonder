Get Started

This platform allows teachers and students to share and explore references and educational material. In EduWonder, one can collect information found on the internet and then share it with the members of previously created groups, which offers the possibility to manage more effectively the academic content found online, improve research techniques, and have a digital record of what students achieved during the course. Likewise, it provides the opportunity for teachers to organize a virtual class with their students.This social platform makes ir easy for the teachers and students to work and develop together.
